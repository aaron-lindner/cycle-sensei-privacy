Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
The short version, warrior: everything stays on your device. No account. No analytics. No servers. No data collection of any kind. What happens in the dojo stays in the dojo.

Overview

Cycle Sensei is designed with your privacy in mind. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

Data We Collect

The app stores the following information — locally on your device only:

  • Partner's name
  • Cycle start date and length
  • Daily mission streak, honor, and progression stats
  • Notification preferences
  • Optional profile photo (never leaves your photo library and device)

None of this information is collected by us or shared with anyone. We have no servers, and we could not see your data even if we wanted to.

How We Use Your Data

All information entered into Cycle Sensei is used solely to calculate cycle phases and deliver daily advice on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Data Storage & Deletion

All data is stored in local app storage on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data. There is nothing to delete anywhere else, because nothing is stored anywhere else.

Notifications & Third-Party Services

Daily reminders are local notifications scheduled entirely on your device. Cycle Sensei uses no push notification service, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party data services.

Children's Privacy

Cycle Sensei is intended for users aged 18 and over.

Medical Disclaimer

Cycle Sensei is for entertainment and education. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or contraception guidance, and its cycle estimates are approximate. For health concerns, consult a qualified professional.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and reflected in the app. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
